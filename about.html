About Me

My Story

In my work I wish to marry a love of tech and design with a dedication towards serving the greater good. Looking toward this goal, I received my BA in Media Arts and have served as an educator for the past six years. Based in Albuquerque, NM, I am currently seeking new opportunities in UI/UX Design. In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, good books, good coffee, and, of course, a healthy obsession with Star Wars.
